COMP 3104 DevOps is a course offered at George Brown College designed to introduce students to the practices and tools involved in DevOps. DevOps is a collaborative approach that integrates development and operations teams to improve software delivery. This course covers essential topics such as continuous integration, continuous delivery, infrastructure as code, automation, and monitoring. Students also learn about popular DevOps tools like Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes, and gain hands-on experience in setting up CI/CD pipelines and automated testing environments.
