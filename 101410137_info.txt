George Brown College (GBC) is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1967 and offers more than 160 full-time programs, including diplomas, degrees, and postgraduate certificates. GBC focuses on career-oriented learning, with strong ties to various industries, helping students gain hands-on experience through co-op placements and industry projects. The college has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront, each housing specialized programs and facilities.
